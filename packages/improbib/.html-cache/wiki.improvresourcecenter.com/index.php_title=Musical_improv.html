Musical improv

From IRC Improv Wiki
Jump to: navigation, search

Musical improv is improv where lyrics and/or melodies are created in the moment.

In musical improv, performers create all of the lyrics on the spot most often with live music accompaniment. This includes short-form games and long-form shows that attempt to re-create musical theater using improv.

New York Musical Improv Festival

The New York Musical Improv Festival (NYMIF) is a four-day festival held at the Magnet Theater in New York City.
