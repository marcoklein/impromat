Demand a Dance

From IRC Improv Wiki
Jump to: navigation, search

Players in a circle. One player points at another and names a fictional dance ("Snappy Penguin," "Grabbing Some Bread," "The Angry Wheelbarrow"). The player pointed at must create a dance inspired by that name, and the other members of the group copy as it's being done. After a few moments, another player (neither the one who made up that dance or the dancer) will point at someone else and name another ficitonal dance.

Purpose

To be silly. To be instantly inspired by a suggestion. To wake up physically.

History

Brian Berrebbi made this up while on Monkeydick.
